Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Legal Assist account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what data will be removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send an email to saqibali.0748@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
2
Include the email address associated with your Legal Assist account so we can identify it
3

Send Deletion Request

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (email, name, nationality, country of residence)
  • All legal cases and conversation history
  • Subscription and payment records
  • All personal preferences and settings

Data Retention

Some anonymized, non-identifiable data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention purposes before being permanently purged. No personally identifiable information is retained after the deletion period.
