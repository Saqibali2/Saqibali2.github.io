Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

Legal Assist is built by Saqib Ali (Built by Saqib). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Legal Assist mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Legal Assist:

  • Account information: Email address, password (encrypted), nationality, and country of residence
  • Profile information: Your selected role (Citizen, Lawyer, or Law Student) and preferred language
  • Case data: Legal questions, case descriptions, and AI-generated responses you create within the app
  • Usage data: App interactions, feature usage, and session information for improving the service
  • Payment information: Subscription status (payment processing is handled by third-party providers; we do not store card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized AI legal guidance based on your nationality and country of residence
  • Create and manage your legal cases and conversation history
  • Send account-related emails (verification, password reset)
  • Process subscription payments and manage your plan
  • Improve our AI models and app features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on DigitalOcean managed infrastructure located in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • Secure managed database with access controls
  • Regular security monitoring

4. AI and Third-Party Services

Legal Assist uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Your legal queries are processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate responses. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.
  • Resend: Used for transactional email delivery (verification emails)
  • DigitalOcean: Cloud infrastructure and database hosting

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the app (listed above)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Legal Disclaimer

Legal Assist provides AI-generated legal information for educational and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional legal advice from a licensed attorney. Always consult a qualified lawyer for formal legal representation or specific legal decisions.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your case data
